Battle City: a two-player PvP version on the DE10 FPGA board using SystemVerilog

Jiaming Xu*, Rui Gong*
Designed players-control tanks via keyboard input, each starting from different positions, aiming to destroy their opponent’s headquarters.
Achieved tank movements dynamic and bullet firing mechanics, and several different functional blocks, closely resembling the classic game’s experience.

Reaction Wheel Pendulum

Jiaming Xu*, Haocheng Yang* Qiran Pang*
Conducted mathematical modeling and applied the Lagrangian approach for system analysis.
Implemented and optimized PID control algorithms for friction compensation.
Designed and simulated observer-based control systems, achieving effective stabilization of the inverted pendulum.
Conducted modeling, simulation, and control systems analysis with MATLAB and Simulink.
